Decoy Bar is located in Toronto’s Corktown neighbourhood, just east of the financial district and north of the Distillery.

Our draught menu changes frequently and features Ontario beers and ciders from the province's most outstanding producers.

Our fridge is stocked with Belgian beers, ready-to-drink cocktails, ciders, and non-alcoholic beverages. See our current menu of bottles, cans, and wine, or check out our cocktail list.

We don't serve food, but food from other establishments can be consumed in the bar.

Our backyard patio is open from May to October.
