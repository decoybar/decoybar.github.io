Decoy Bar is located in Toronto’s Corktown neighbourhood, just east of the financial district and north of the Distillery.

Our draught menu features outstanding Ontario beers (and sometimes ciders), with a focus on beers that aren’t commonly available from retailers. We stock our fridge with Belgian beers, ready-to-drink cocktails, ciders, and non-alcoholic beverages. We also have a small but growing list of wine and cocktails.

Our backyard patio is open from May to October.
